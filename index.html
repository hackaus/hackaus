What are Hackathons?

Hackathons are competitions that challenge people to create something over a set time period using technologies.

Why we do them

We believe hackathons are a fantastic way to learn from each other. Ideate, validate, and build a protype. They're educational, engaging, empowering.

What we do

As the driving force to accelerate an innovative culture in Australia, our focus is to facilitate and empower hackathons and equip them with the right skills, people, education and processes to ensure it is a success.
