About us

Hackathons Australia is a community group that empowers hackathons to have the right skills, people, partners, education and processes to ensure it is a success. To be informed about the hackathons, complete this form.

What we do

Help Hackathon organisers attract participants by being a Community Partner.
Let participants know which Hackathons are happening in their area.
Help partners/sponsors to be involved in the innovation ecosystem.
Build a community of people which get sh*t done and make a difference.

Contact us to find out more!
